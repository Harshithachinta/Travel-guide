South Korea

South Korea is known for its vibrant culture, K-pop, delicious food, and modern cities.

  • Capital: Seoul
  • Famous Places: Gyeongbokgung Palace, Busan Beaches, Jeju Island
  • Special Food: Kimchi, Bibimbap, Tteokbokki
  • Best Time to Visit: March to May, September to November

Thailand

Thailand is famous for its tropical beaches, temples, nightlife, and street food.

  • Capital: Bangkok
  • Famous Places: Phuket, Pattaya, Chiang Mai
  • Special Food: Pad Thai, Tom Yum Soup, Green Curry
  • Best Time to Visit: November to February

Japan

Japan is a perfect blend of tradition and technology, known for cherry blossoms and discipline.

  • Capital: Tokyo
  • Famous Places: Kyoto, Mount Fuji, Osaka
  • Special Food: Sushi, Ramen, Tempura
  • Best Time to Visit: March to May, October to November

Singapore

Singapore is a clean, modern city-state known for luxury, shopping, and food culture.

  • Capital: Singapore
  • Famous Places: Marina Bay Sands, Sentosa Island, Gardens by the Bay
  • Special Food: Hainanese Chicken Rice, Laksa, Chilli Crab
  • Best Time to Visit: February to April
